ABOUT US

NCS Logistics and Distribution Private Limited NCS Logistics and Distribution Private Limited is a company, established in 2002 and is registered under the Company Act, 1956. It is a growing organization in the field of Express distribution. Its core competency is Local distribution, Mumbai & its suburbs, Bhiwandi, Nashik Pune. NCS Logistics strength is its ownership of over 50+ vehicles and more than 200 attached vehicles. NCS Logistics and Distribution Pvt. Ltd. was established by  Mr. Ulhas Phatak over 2 decades ago as a small proprietorship firm with meagre resources and dependency on hired infrastructure. However, the path of selfless & sheer hard work put in by Mr. Phatak and his dedicated team of professionals with natural talent has led to becoming what NCS Logistics and Distributions Pvt. Ltd. is today. A brand recognized for its quality service NCS has steadily grown both horizontally and vertically resulting in becoming an enviable entity in the business of logistics. Our company entered into the corporate arena by registering under the Company Act in 2002, a period when even our stronger competitors were reluctant. Today, apart from establishing a strong and vibrant infrastructure with our own massive resources of fleet of vehicles our extremely efficient manpower has helped us become one of the largest Intra-City and Inter suburbs Delivery Network. NCS Logistics here plays a very important role in servicing various industries & individuals by keeping abreast with the industry standards and delivering what we promise. We have invested our time and financial resources to create a system that will adapt to all your needs. With the new addition of our internet service we are prepared to move into the 21st century with all the confidence you expect from your transportation provider in a techno savvy manner.

Our Network

NCS have centralized hubs located in Mumbai with multiple branches located all over Mumbai and Bhiwandi to pickup and deliver Shipment.

Our Mission

To promote teamwork & create a work environment encouraging the workforce to continuously strive for quality & excellence, providing high-quality service to customers under one roof.

Our Values

Core values that drive the culture of NCS Logistics are Agility, integrity, ownership, teamwork and excellence. We seek, encourage, celebrate and reward these values because they are essential to the promises we’ve made to our customers, vendors, employees, communities, and environment.

Vision

NCS Logistics seeks to be a premier, profitable provider of global supply-chain services to help enable sustainable trade and commerce in key markets and regions. We will accomplish this by investing in our people, our facilities and in technology. We will continue to build and nurture a culture promoting challenge, inquiry and a continual focus on the customer.

Quality Policy

NCS Logistics follows stringent qualty control and checks to ensure that the best of the services are provided to the customers at all times.
